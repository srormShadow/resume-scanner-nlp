The role also requires a strong grasp of application security, secure data handling, and safeguarding practices.
Experience with monorepo structures, package management, memory optimization, and performance enhancement is considered a plus. The candidate should be capable of designing medium to complex features involving multiple teams. Professional experience working in Agile and Scrum projects is mandatory, along with strong teamwork, collaborative problem solving skills, effective task prioritization, and the ability to work independently with minimal supervision.
